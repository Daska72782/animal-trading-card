African Lion

Apex predators at the top of the food chain, but not great hunters.The females are usually the ones who do all the hunting for their pride.

  • Scientific name: Panthera Leo
  • Average Length: 8-9ft
  • Diet: medium and large herbivores
  • Habitat: grasslands, woodlands, and thick brush

Lion's are highly social cats that live in prides of 30-40 members. The african lion is a member of the genus panthera and also joining tigers, jaguars and leopard family. A lion's roar can be heard from 5 miles away. The average weight of a male lion is 570 lbs. Male lions stay in their birth pride for 2 years then are kicked out. A lion's success rate of hunt is less than 30%, so that means that the female usually does the hunting. Their average lifespan is typically about 10-15 years on an average.
